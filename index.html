Book Friend

Bookfriend can give you a synopsis of any book you either love or want to learn more about. It can also recommend similar books you might enjoy so you will never have to ask yourself "what do I read now?"

How it works:

Enter a book title and click search to find basic information about the book, including a synopsis, author, etc.

Click on recommendations to get a list of books that are similar to the title you entered. Then select it from the list to learn more about it.
